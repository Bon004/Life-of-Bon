## Section 1 — Intentional Creative Forks
*(From master doc Section 12 — decide when the story demands it, not before)*

- [ ] **#1 — World name:** Aetheria vs. Eldarion. One becomes the world name; the other becomes a region, historical name, or is cut. Decide before line-editing the manuscript.
- [ ] **#2 — Elena's enslavement (who/where):** Leon is dead, Elena is enslaved — that's locked. Still open: who enslaved her and under what conditions? Options: human warlord/noble on Dorwin (political captive); criminal underground (sold in the chaos); foreign kingdom that absorbed refugees (trapped, not free to leave).
- [ ] **#3 — Eternal Sentinel counterforce:** Does Vaela's counterforce develop into its own named character or faction? Let early arc drafting reveal which angle lands best.
- [ ] **#4 — Bon's final ending:** Four options open — (a) killed by a hero; (b) retires to old age; (c) dies saving the love interest (Aki bookend); (d) **favorite: dies with a genuine smile, becomes a legend.** Leave open until the trajectory of his growth is fully felt.
- [ ] **#5 — Names still TBD:** Best friend, love interest, mercenary band. Vaela is locked. Tragic Champion role is suspended (not just unnamed).
- [ ] **#6 — Bon's unique magical signature:** Leading idea: visually impure — unstable edges, color shifts with emotional state, "works but looks wrong by academic standards." Not yet locked.
- [ ] **#7 — Mentor figure (Arc 3):** Who are they? What is their backstory? Rock Lee archetype — earned everything through work, not talent.
- [ ] **#8 — Mercenary band name:** What is the band called? What are they known for?
- [ ] **#9 — First tragic romance (Arc 3):** Who is she? How does it end — her death, or Bon's self-sabotage?
- [ ] **#10 — When/how does Bon meet his best friend?** Options: mercenary years, solo adventurer period, Academy arc. A childhood friendship pre-disaster would require a later reunion — possible but needs deliberate design.
- [ ] **#11 — The Demon Lords:** Who specifically appears in Bon's story, in which arc, and under what circumstances? At least one will appear directly. TBD.
- [ ] **#12 — Bon's crash out:** The accumulation that finally breaks through. When does it happen exactly, and what specific weight tips it? Left open to be discovered through writing.
